Our Mission and Vision

Mission
Our mission at the Oasis Community Sports Center is to support children from vulnerable families by providing them with opportunities to engage in various sports and recreational activities. Through the practice of handball, volleyball, basketball, table tennis, mini football, and traditional dances and music, we aim to: Foster a love for sports and healthy living. Encourage school retention and academic success. Promote values of fair play, teamwork, and respect. Build a supportive and inclusive community. !

Vision
To create a vibrant and inclusive community where all children, regardless of their background, have access to sports and recreational activities that promote physical health, personal growth, and social well-being.

Most of our events have hard and easy route choices as we are always keen to encourage new riders.
